PT Harapan Abadi Tekstil Indonesia, also known as HATI, is a future leading textile manufacturer located in Pasuruan, Indonesia. Focused on providing reliable, sustainable and high quality products with our finest material. We appreciate design as an art, by passion of our skilled designers and artisans to deliver the premium solution to meet our customer demand.

Our passion to produce high quality products is matched only by installing the latest modern, high performance and technology textile equipment. Nothing is extraneous for us, every detail matters.
